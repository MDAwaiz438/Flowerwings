## Project brief

**Client:** Flower Wings — online flower & gift delivery, Bangalore  
**Goal:** Complete redesign. Premium feel, mobile-first, same logo colors.  
**Primary users:** Mobile shoppers in Bangalore gifting for occasions  
**Core action:** Browse → Pick occasion → Add to cart → Checkout → Track  

---

## The one-line design principle

> *Pink and green stay exactly as the logo. Everything else — spacing, type, restraint — does the work of making it feel premium.*

---

## What "premium" means here

Not dark, not minimal-to-the-point-of-empty. Premium means:

- Every element earns its place
- Nothing looks like a template
- Interactions feel considered, not bolted on
- The brand color (#FF83A8) is used with intention, not everywhere
- Typography creates emotion before a single photo loads
- Mobile experience is faster and more delightful than competitors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
